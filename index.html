Process

The same process, every time

Research the real problem first

Pick an industry, research what's actually broken for the target user today, using real market/regulatory data, before inventing a product.

Build personas & run synthetic interviews

Ground personas in that real research; interview them (AI-generated, clearly labeled synthetic) to pressure-test assumptions before roadmapping.

Prioritize with a defensible framework

ICE or RICE, chosen deliberately per project, every score tied to a specific persona, JTBD, or interview finding, not a gut call.

Prototype it

A clickable, coded prototype of the MVP flow, not just wireframes, so the product is something you can actually use, not just read about.

(In progress) Prove it's buildable and trackable

Layering in feasibility notes, adoption/PMF tracking, and success metrics on top of the case studies that warrant it.

All products shown here are fictional case studies built for portfolio purposes. Market research is real and cited; personas, interviews, and user data are synthetic.
