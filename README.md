# Shubha's PM Portfolio

B2B2C product case studies built to demonstrate product management process: research, personas, roadmapping, prototyping, and documentation.

## Contents

- `index.html` - portfolio hub
- `contact.html` - contact page
- `projects/flexy/` - Flexy, a plug-in for ComEd customers combining live dynamic-pricing visibility with automated EV charging. See `projects/flexy/README.md` for what's inside.

Each case study lives in its own folder under `projects/`. As new case studies (SunnySideUp, and others from the roadmap) are built out, they land in their own `projects/<name>/` folder here rather than a separate repo.

Every case study runs on an invented company and product. The market research behind each one is real and cited; personas, interviews, and user data are synthetic.
